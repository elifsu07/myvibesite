What are the benefits of iodine?

24/08/24


Benefits of Iodine Salt: Why Is It Important for Your Health?


Iodine is an important mineral required for the healthy functioning of our body. It is especially vital for the proper functioning of the thyroid gland. Iodine deficiency can lead to serious health problems such as goiter. Here are the benefits of iodine salt for our health:

  • Thyroid health: Iodine is essential for the production of thyroid hormones. These hormones play an important role in many body functions such as regulation of metabolism, growth and development.
  • Brain development: Iodine is very important for brain development, especially during infancy and childhood. Deficiency can cause problems such as learning disabilities and mental retardation.
  • Energy metabolism: Iodine regulates the way the body uses energy. Deficiency can lead to fatigue, weakness and weight gain.
  • Immune system: Iodine helps strengthen the immune system and protects the body against infections.
  • Heart health: Iodine is also important for heart health. It helps regulate heart rate and keep blood pressure under control.

What are the symptoms of iodine deficiency?


  • Tiredness
  • Depression
  • Weakness
  • Weight gain
  • Hair loss
  • Skin dryness
  • Constipation
  • Additionally, iodine deficiency can lead to cancer.

Iodine Supplements and Foods Containing Iodine

Iodine taken from salts is often insufficient for us. We can get iodine from marine creatures such as fish, oysters, shrimp, lobster and seaweed.

Iodine supplements can be taken with the recommendation of a doctor.

Important Notes:

Although experts say that we should not be afraid of excess iodine, iodine supplements should not be used without a doctor's advice.
The need for iodine increases during pregnancy and breastfeeding. During this period, iodine intake should be ensured under the supervision of a doctor.
